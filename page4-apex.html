Apex Legends

Battle Royale with Teamwork and Legendary Characters

Released by Respawn Entertainment in 2019, Apex Legends redefined battle royale with its focus on 3-player squads, unique "Legends," and fast-paced movement.

Legends & Teamwork

30+ Legends with distinct passive, tactical, and ultimate abilities. For example, Wraith can phase through walls, while Gibraltar deploys a defensive dome. Success depends on synergizing squad members’ skills.

Gameplay Mechanics

Matches start with 60 players (20 squads) dropping onto a shrinking map. Players loot weapons, armor, and items, then fight to be the last squad standing. The "Apex Jump" and sliding mechanics add dynamic movement.

Live Service & Updates

Free-to-play with seasonal "Battles Passes" (cosmetics and rewards). Regular updates introduce new Legends, maps, and game modes (like "Control" or "Arenas"), keeping the game fresh year-round.
